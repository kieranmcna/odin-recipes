Berry Breakfast Drink

Description

A refreshing morning drink to start your day off the right way.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup chilled orange juice
  • ⅓ cup chilled pineapple juice
  • 2 cups vanilla yogurt
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • ½ cup frozen sliced strawberries
  • ½ banana, sliced

Steps

  1. Place the orange juice, pineapple juice, yogurt, blueberries, strawberries, and bananas into a blender. Cover and blend until smooth. The berry drink will be very thick. Serve immediately.
